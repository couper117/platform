# RwaSport — 2-Minute Pitch

"Every day, thousands of Rwandan athletes and teams compete in leagues, but the management process is still stuck in paper and spreadsheets. 

RwaSport is the country's first truly digital sports ecosystem. We don't just show scores—we empower federations and schools to manage their entire operations. With RwaSport, League Admins can manage thousands of matches, Team Managers can verify their players in minutes, and match reporters can broadcast live events from the pitch in real-time. 

But RwaSport is also a business. We generate revenue through verified team subscriptions and premium advertising spots within our high-traffic live match centers. 

Whether it's the professional Premier League or the next generation in the Kagame Cup, RwaSport brings professional-grade management, revenue, and digital transformation to the entire nation. We aren't just an app—we are the digital backbone of Rwandan sports."
