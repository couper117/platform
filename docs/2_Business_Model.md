# RwaSport — Business Model

## 1. Value Proposition
RwaSport digitizes the fragmented sports ecosystem in Rwanda. It provides transparency, data-driven insights for federations, a streamlined verification process for teams, and real-time engagement for fans.

## 2. Revenue Streams
- **B2B SaaS (Team Verification Fees):** Teams pay an annual verification fee to participate in official leagues. Verified status grants access to league matches, official document management, and national rankings.
- **In-Platform Advertising:**
  - **Match-Day Sponsorship:** Premium ad slots in the Live Reporting portal.
  - **Banner Placements:** Strategic ad positions on the homepage and news feeds for local partners.
- **Transaction Processing:** Fee-based processing for player transfers managed through the platform.

## 3. Growth Strategy
- **AKC3 (Schools):** Digitizing school sports creates long-term user retention, training thousands of future athletes and managers to use RwaSport from a young age.
- **Sponsorship Partnerships:** Targeting companies interested in the youth sports demographic.

## 4. Operational Excellence
- Scalable cloud infrastructure (Serverless) keeps overhead low during off-seasons.
- Automated verification reduces manual administration burden for federations.
